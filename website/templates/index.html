About Our System

Our System is an intelligent, web-based Blood Donation Management Platform designed to unify and streamline every step of the donation lifecycle. By combining real-time inventory tracking with a centralized donor database, our platform automatically matches eligible “Blood Heroes” to urgent “Blood Fighter” requests using blood‐group compatibility, GPS proximity, and donor availability. Donors manage their profiles, receive automated eligibility reminders, and hold digital QR-coded donor cards, while patients submit structured requests and track fulfillment status. Behind the scenes, our ML-powered demand forecasting anticipates shortages and optimizes appointment scheduling, and administrators gain full visibility through dynamic dashboards, expiry alerts, and inter-bank coordination tools. With geolocation-enabled maps of nearby centers and campaigns, intelligent notifications, and personalized donation reports, our system transforms fragmented manual processes into a predictive, responsive, and user-centric network saving more lives through faster, smarter blood management.
